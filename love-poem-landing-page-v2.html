Whispers of the Heart

In the quiet moments between breaths,

I find you — my forever, my now.

Your laughter is the melody that dances

through the chambers of my soul.

With you, time becomes a gentle tide,

pulling us closer with each passing moment.

Your eyes hold constellations I could get lost in

for eternity and still crave more.

You are the calm in my storms,

the warmth in my winters,

Always and forever,

yours completely.
